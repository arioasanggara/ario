About Me

Food Consultant Culinary Arts • At-Sunrice GlobalChef Academy Singapore

Growing and Scaling Your F&B Brands.

My culinary journey began in childhood, shaped by the presence of my mother in the kitchen—where cooking was not merely a routine, but an expression of care and dedication. From those early moments, I developed a deep appreciation for the craft, which gradually evolved into a lifelong passion.

For me, cooking is more than the act of preparing food; it is about creating experiences. Every dish is an opportunity to evoke emotion, to tell a story, and to leave a lasting impression. The true reward lies in witnessing others connect with what I create—finding joy, comfort, and meaning in every bite.

Driven by this philosophy, I continue to refine my approach to the culinary arts—balancing precision, consistency, and creativity—to deliver not just meals, but thoughtful and memorable dining experiences.

Helping F&B businesses build structured systems, consistent product quality, and profit strategies that actually work in real operations — not just in theory.

Graduated with Major in Culinary Arts from At-Sunrice GlobalChef Academy Singapore. Backed by hands-on experience from CÉ LA VI Singapore to leading multi-brand cloud kitchen operations.
